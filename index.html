About me




Moch Rizki Kurniawan

"If the plan doesn't work, change the plan but never the goal"

Hello! My name is Moch Rizki was born in Bandung, June 21, 1999. I love digital art, since I was a child I really like drawing and love colors. My hobby is photography, maybe you could say the thing I like best is taking pictures and editing photos and videos, when I am bored or stuck with assignments from school I have to do things that make my mood come back again by editing or listening to music. I am a persistent and hard worker, curious and disciplined in doing something, I really like the creative world. Since junior high school really followed extracurricular technology and information from there I started learning about the world of computers, entering vocational high school I got to know the world of design and photography I was very interested in this field, then I started to learn other things in the creative world such as learning programming computer and editing photos and videos, there I realized that my talent in the creative industry, maybe not too smart but I am someone who wants to keep learning. My weakness is that sometimes I always work too hard, sometimes to the point of sacrificing my rest and weekends to make sure my assignments are completed before deadlines are met. but I can still overcome this on the weekend to freshen my mind so that I can be fresh back to work later. maybe that's at least about my personality. thank you !!
